Understanding Traffic Accidents

Our system aims to collect and analyze data on traffic fatalities to improve road safety. By understanding the causes and patterns of accidents, we can help reduce the number of casualties on our roads.

Key Statistics

  • Annual Road Fatalities: Approximately 1.3 million people die each year in road accidents globally.
  • Major Causes: Speeding, Distracted Driving, Drunk Driving, Weather Conditions, and Reckless Driving.
  • Impact: Traffic accidents are the leading cause of death for people aged 5-29 years.
  • Data Utilization: Our AI system uses historical data to predict accident hotspots and provide preventive measures.

System Features

Data Collection

Gather data from various sources, including police reports and hospital records, for comprehensive analysis.

Data Analysis

Utilize AI to identify patterns and trends in traffic accidents for predictive modeling.

Easy access to information and reports with a well-designed user interface.
